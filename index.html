UX: User Design

UX design is concerned with usability, content, and interaction.

Color Choices

When thinking about color choices, consider the psychology of colors. Soft and friendly colors like light blue, tan, and turquoise are a good choice for family law websites. Colors like gray, black, white, and dark blue can be used for business law websites.Even images should match your website's color scheme. Try using a professional headshot that includes a touch of your website's colors in elements like clothing and office furniture.

UX: User Design Strategy

UX strategy is the process that should be started before the design or development of a digital product. UX strategy is the 'Big Picture'. It is the plan to acheieve one or more business goals.

A solid UX strategy will help you design a digital product that users want. Just throwing a website on the internet with no thought process is not recommended. If you wouldn't open a business without a plan, why would you showcase your business online without one?

UI: User Interface

The purpose of user interface is to enable a user to effectively contol a computer or machine they are interacting with. A successful user interface should be easy and enjoyable to use.

UI Elements

Input Controls: checkboxes, dropdown list, and buttons

Navigational Components: search fields and icons

Information Components: progress bars, notifications, and message boxes
